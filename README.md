# ✝️ Tap N Pray

**One Tap. One Verse. One Step Closer to Christ.**

🌐 **Live Site:** https://tapnpray.github.io/

---

## 📖 Overview

**Tap N Pray** is a beautifully designed, distraction-free web app that helps you engage with Scripture daily.

With a single tap, you receive a thoughtfully selected Bible verse you can:
- Read
- Listen to
- Reflect on
- Share with others

The experience is intentionally simple, immersive, and centered on God's Word.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 📅 Daily Verse (Smart + Consistent)
A new verse every day, consistent across all users, powered by a **weighted curated system**:
- High-impact verses appear more often
- Encouraging and faith-building passages are prioritized
- Seasonal verses (Christmas, Easter) are dynamically boosted

---

### 🔀 Random Verse Mode
Explore Scripture freely beyond the daily verse.

---

### 🔊 Read Aloud (Text-to-Speech)
Tap the verse to listen:
- Smooth word-by-word highlighting
- Calm, natural pacing
- Designed for reflection and focus

---

### 🌿 Ambient Sound Mode
Optional background audio to create a peaceful environment for:
- Prayer
- Meditation
- Quiet time with Scripture

---

### 🔁 Streak Tracking System
Stay consistent in your daily walk:

- Tracks daily engagement
- Encourages habit formation
- Stored locally for privacy

## 🧠 Advanced Systems (Under the Hood)

### 🎯 Weighted Verse Engine
Verses are not random—they are:
- Categorized by impact
- Assigned weights (3–5)
- Selected using a deterministic daily seed

### 📆 Seasonal Intelligence
Automatically surfaces relevant Scripture during:
- Christmas
- Easter (dynamic date calculation)
